# Asset Descriptions for Landing Page Generation

## Project Theme

**Theme:** "Steampunk Goblin Cartel" / "Victorian Fantasy Finance"
**General Aesthetic:** Ornate, wealthy, slightly mischievous, combining 19th-century luxury with fantasy elements.

---

## Image Reference: `banner_social.webp`

**Role:** Hero Background / Storytelling Section

### Visual Description

A horizontal, highly detailed illustration depicting a negotiation scene in a lavish Victorian parlor.

- **Characters:**
  - **Left:** A large, broad-shouldered Orc with olive-green skin, wearing a blue brocade coat with gold embroidery, a white ruffled shirt, and a jeweled ascot. He is serious and stacking gold coins.
  - **Right:** A smaller, cunning Goblin with green skin, wearing a gold/cream patterned vest, a red cravat, and a top hat with a monocle. He is sipping tea with a sly expression.
- **Objects:** An ornate blue and gold teapot, delicate teacups, stacks of gold coins, candlelight.
- **Lighting:** Warm, dim, ambient interior lighting (candlelight).

### Color Palette (Hex Codes Approx.)

- **Primary:** Midnight Blue , Antique Gold
- **Secondary:** Olive Green , Deep Red
- **Highlight:** Candlelight Yellow

### Technical Implementation for Web

- **CSS Filters:** Slight warm vignette to focus center attention.
- **Usage:** Best used as a wide background for an "About the Cartel" section or the main Hero image with text overlaid in the center-left or center-right negative space.

## Image Reference: `goblin_sam.webp`

**Role:** Call to Action (CTA) / Mascot Avatar

### Visual Description

A portrait-oriented, sticker-style character illustration with a plain background.

- **Subject:** The Goblin character from `banner_social.webp` leaning forward aggressively.
- **Action:** Pointing a sharp, clawed finger directly at the viewer (reminiscent of "Uncle Sam").
- **Attire:** Top hat with a red rose, monocle, dark suit with gold trim.
- **Style:** Comic book inking, heavy outlines, cel-shaded.

### Color Palette (Hex Codes Approx.)

- **Primary:** Acid Green , Charcoal Black
- **Accent:** Ruby Red
- **Metal:** Silver/Grey

### Technical Implementation for Web

- **Layout:** Isolate the character from the background (remove background).
- **Usage:** Place floating over the edge of a container or button. Ideal for the "Join Now" or "Subscribe" section to break the grid layout.
- **Animation:** Slight hover effect (scale up 1.05x) to emphasize the pointing gesture.

## Image Reference: `clt_banner_alt.webp`

**Role:** Main Logo / Header Branding

### Visual Description

A decorative, badge-style graphic featuring typography and iconography.

- **Text:** "CARTEL" (Arched, Red with Gold trim) and "LUCROS TAXADOS" (Straight, larger, Red with Gold extrusion).
- **Font Style:** Vintage Circus / Western Serif.
- **Iconography:** Central top hat with a red rose and monocle resting on a gold rune coin. Surrounded by gears, gems, and ledgers.
- **Border:** Art Nouveau style frame in green and gold.

### Color Palette (Hex Codes Approx.)

- **Typography:** Crimson Red , Metallic Gold
- **Background:** Parchment Beige , Hunter Green

### Technical Implementation for Web

- **Usage:** Center of the Navigation Bar or as the primary branding element at the top of the page.
- **Responsiveness:** Ensure the text remains legible on mobile; consider cropping to just the "Top Hat" icon for mobile sticky headers.

## Image Reference: `clt_banner.webp`

**Role:** Footer Background / Wide Banner

### Visual Description

A wide, cinematic banner illustration focusing on abundance and machinery.

- **Text:** "CARTEL LUCROS TAXADOS" in large, 3D gold lettering floating in the center.
- **Setting:** A magical workshop or counting house.
- **Foreground:** Cluttered desks filled with towers of gold coins, open ledger books, brass gears, and steampunk machinery.
- **Atmosphere:** Glowing, magical, with green mist/haze rising from the back.

### Color Palette (Hex Codes Approx.)

- **Primary:** Goldenrod , Wood Brown
- **Atmosphere:** Magic Green
- **Accents:** Jewel Tones (Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald)

### Technical Implementation for Web

- **Usage:** Perfect for the page Footer or a "How it Works" section breaker.
- **Text Overlay:** Since the image already contains the title text, avoid placing HTML text directly over the center. Use the surrounding clutter to frame HTML buttons or links if used as a footer.
